Hello,
I'm Aerin.

I'm currently a senior at NYU Shanghai expected to graduate in May 2026. Originally from Los Angeles, I'm grateful for the opportunity to have studied all over the world including: Paris, New York, and of course Shanghai.

Academically, I focus on data, systems, and emerging technologies. My work has included projects in decentralized identity systems, machine learning, and data analysis, where I'm particularly interested in how technical infrastructure shapes privacy, security, and decision-making. I enjoy translating complex technical ideas into clear explanations, which has also led me to explore technical writing and analytics.

At the same time, I've always been drawn to the creative side of ideas. I enjoy writing, film, and art history, and I'm interested in how technology interacts with culture and media. Whether I'm analyzing a dataset, writing about a new technology, or developing a business idea, I'm motivated by the same goal: turning complex systems into something understandable and useful.

Long term, I'm interested in working where technology, data, and creative industries overlap, in spaces where analytical thinking and storytelling both matter.
